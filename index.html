Many Words At Once Written

Many Words At Once Written is an Emagazine created by students and for students. If you are music or sport's freak, like watching films and playing video games and are interested in current affairs - we invite you to have a look and learn few new things.

Welcome to the third issue of our school Emagazine!

Welcome in the new school year. We are starting with new set of interesting articles and new section. As usual we have something for music fanatics in our music sections. This time we present to you Louisa Johnson and Logic. For those who follow situation on our political scene, we invite you to Politically incorrect section. Film and game fans will be glad to read the review of the film “Arrival” and game “I wanna be the Boshy”. For those who love sport we have some interesting facts about F1 legend Alberto Ascari. And last but not least – our new section Tongue-Twisted where you can read about some useful methods of learning foreign languages.

Remember – if you want to join our group – we are waiting for you!

K. Bugno
