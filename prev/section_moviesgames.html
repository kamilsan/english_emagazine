Movie: Arrival

Arrival is a science-fiction movie about some unidentified flying objects that suddenly appeared on earth in different locations. Sounds like nothing special, just another sci-fi movie about aliens trying to destroy the earth. Well.. not really. The plot tells a story of an university teacher, who was given a job to translate aliens language. After many trials and errors she finally manages to understand them, and that's when the fun part begins. I don't want to make a spoiler, but there are many things happening which will definitely frustrate you. It's a kind of movie that makes you think and predict what is going to happen, and after all your efforts, it gives you unexpected answers. Not much action but it's definitely worth watching.
