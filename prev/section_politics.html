Presidents Vetos!

During holidays we could observe big changes in Polish political scene. Government decided that they wanted to get rid of post-PRL’s judges, who are mostly placed in high ranks of judiciary system. They had written 3 new laws and tried to push them through Parliament. Opposition immediately started protests and national campaign, in order to stop those laws. Despite this, the government didn’t want to abandon their laws, which was a part of great campaign of decommunization, and started opposite campaign in television and Internet, praising current government and spreading hate against judges and opposition. But what was the problem? New laws were declining the power of Highest Court, giving the whole power to Attorney General, who is nominated by the Prime Minister. And here we have a simple conclusion, that if the reform of judiciary system became official, we would destroy the rule of tripartite division of power, and this situation could open a wide way to many abuses. Moreover, European Parliament denounced our government’s decision about reforming judiciary system, as well as many of law doctors. That’s why president Andrzej Duda used veto law and sent two out of three projects of laws to reconsideration. Both opposition and government politicians were shocked. Media loyal to the PiS immediately started the campaign against the president, and many of politicians criticized his decision, especially Zbigniew Ziobro, the minister of justice and the Attorney General at the same time. President also announced that he will prepare his version of laws and present it in September. Now, we have a status quo between opposition, government and president, and after presidential presentation we will be able to recognize which side of conflict is right. In the meantime, we have a very interesting situation, in which the president stands against his mother-party, and is successful in it. It’s also a sign, that he has a slightly different vision of Poland then the ruling party. In our reality, where duopoly of PO and PiS is concreted, new fresh party can do really good job, balancing between left and right side. Andrzej Duda since the beginning of his presidency has maintained good relations with politics from Kukiz’15, who are more modern and flexible then PiS’s members, yet still contain traditionalism in their postulates. Now, we can only wait and observe. In a few days we should get information about president’s project and rewritten reforms made by government. Stay tuned.
