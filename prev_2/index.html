Many Words At Once Written

Many Words At Once Written is an Emagazine created by students and for students. If you are music or sport's freak, like watching films and playing video games and are interested in current affairs - we invite you to have a look and learn few new things.

Welcome to the first issue of our school Emagazine!

As we have so many talented students who are very good at English, it seemed a very good idea to use their talent and knowledge to create a school newspaper written by students for students but in English. This is how our small editorial board came to life. At the moment we consist of 5 writers and two webdevelopers ;) We’ve decided to write about things that are interesting for you: music, sport, computer games but also current affairs. All the articles that you will have a chance to read were written by your fellow students, who chose the topics interesting for them. We hope that you’ll enjoy what we have prepared and come back next month to read the second issue!

In this issue we propose some interesting facts concerning recent interest of China in football, the question about Trump and his future as the new US President, music journey with Panic! at the Disco and Quebonafide and a review of game “Half life” and film “Cabin in the woods”.

So please enjoy and come back next month!

K. Bugno
