Panic! At the Disco

Panic! At the Disco is an American rock band. They're from Las Vegas and the band was formed in 2004. Members of this group are : Brendon Urie, who is the lead singer, Dallon Weekes who is the bassist, Kenneth Harris who is the guitarist and the drummer Dan Pawlovich.

The first studio album of Panic! At the Disco was “A fever You can't sweat out” and it was released in 2005. The most recognizable track from the debut album is for sure “I Write Sins Not Tragedies” which won has plenty of awards. The album was certified double platinum in The United States.

The second album which was released in 2008 is called “Pretty. Odd”. In 2010 the third studio album was released. It was “Visces & Virtues” and it was on the 7th place in U.S. Billboard list of 200 best tracks back then.

In 2013 the band released best-selling album “Too Weird to Live, Too Rare to Die!” The most known tracks from this album are definately “Nicotine” and “This is Gospel”. Run of luck of the band lead to completely sold out tour. After such big success, in 2016 the band released absolutely phenomenal fifth studio album called “Death of a Bachelor”. More people heard about Panic! At the Disco because of this outstanding album and there is no need to be surprised about that. You can listen to songs listed down and then you'll know why they became so successful.

Currently the band is touring across U.S. as they European tour finished last year. Here you can listen to some of the best known tracks of Panic! At the Disco. Hope you'll enjoy it :)
